anime

anime is an art form that comprises many genres found in other mediums; it is sometimes mistakenly classified as a genre itself. In Japanese, the term anime is used to refer to all animated works, regardless of style or origin.Anime is a style of animation popular in Japanese films and television series. It often combines stark, colorful graphics with action-packed plots. Early anime films were intended primarily for a Japanese audience. Therefore, they used many cultural references unique to Japan. The term anime [a·nuh·mei] is a Japanese colloquialism used as an abbreviation for the term “animation.” Generally in Japan, the word anime (written アニメ) is synonymous with animation of any kind from anywhere.
